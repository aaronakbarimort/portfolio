Aaron Akbari-Mort

Born in San Jose, CA, Aaron's family migrated to Clovis, CA, after his father secured a teaching job with the local school district. Growing up in the hot, dust bowl part of California's Central Valley, Aaron migrated north after high-school to the fog of San Francisco. After 10 years in the Bay Area and a handful of years touring the country playing rock n' roll, Aaron meet his now wife, Sami Akbari, with whom he splits a last name. After a few years in Los Angeles, they both moved out-of-state to Portland, OR, where they currently reside.

Aaron graduated from San Francisco State University with a B.A. in Fine Arts, Painting.

After college, he took up work designing gig posters, album packaging and web graphics for musicians and music related related companies.
